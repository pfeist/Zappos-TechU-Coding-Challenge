The Survivalist's Guide to the Zombie Apocalypse




 Know the basics:

Never leave home unarmed.
Keep moving and reach your destination as quickly as possible.
If you are not carrying a gun, you are unarmed.
The only way to kill a zombie is to sever the connection between the brain and spinal cord. Always shoot for the head, body shots are wasted ammunition.
Sound attracts hordes, make as little as possible.
Every bite infects. Every Time.
Once infected the victim is dead. Show no mercy.
Avoid open spaces, always stay near cover.
Lights and fire are beacons for hordes. Use them at your own risk.
