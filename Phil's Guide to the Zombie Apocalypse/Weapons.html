The Survivalist's Guide to the Zombie Apocalypse
Weapons:

If it doesn't shoot, its no good. Melee weapons like knives or baseball bats should be your absolute last resort. You should never be close enough to a zombie to need these.
Your primary defense should be a ranged weapon, preferably a high calibre semi-automatic rifle. These will be more accurate than their automatic siblings, and are more user-friendly for novice shooters.
If you can find them, always have a backup weapon or two. Ideally, these should consist of a shotgun and a pistol.
This is not a movie, so keep your empty magazines as you will be hard-pressed to replace them.
